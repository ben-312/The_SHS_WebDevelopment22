Flight Styles & Use Cases

FPV isn't a one-size-fits-all hobby. Whether you're chasing high-speed competition or capturing cinematic vistas, your hardware and technique must adapt to your mission.

High Velocity

Racing

Built for pure speed and agility. Pilots navigate through complex gates at speeds exceeding 100mph. This discipline requires high-refresh rate analog or low-latency digital video systems.

5-inch Props 6S Voltage Low Latency
Indoor / Training

Micro & Whoops

The safest entry point for beginners. Ducted propellers allow for indoor flight and proximity training without the risk of heavy damage.

Creative Expression

Freestyle

Combining acrobatics with architectural environments. It's about 'flow' and finding unique lines through gaps. Heavy-duty frames are required for the occasional impact.

Visual Storytelling

Cinematic / Long Range

Equipped with HD cameras like GoPros or built-in O3 systems. Focused on smooth, sweeping movements and exploring vast landscapes with GPS stabilization.

Flight Controller (FC)

The brain of the drone. It processes gyroscope data and pilot inputs thousands of times per second to maintain stability and execute maneuvers.

  • STM32 Microcontrollers
  • MPU6000 Gyroscope Integration
  • UART Port Connectivity
speed

ESC

Electronic Speed Controllers translate the FC's signals into high-current pulses that drive the motors, supporting protocols like DShot1200.

settings_input_component

Motors

Brushless powerhouses measured in KV (RPM per volt). Low KV for high torque, high KV for raw speed.

settings_input_antenna

VTX & Antennas

The Video Transmitter (VTX) sends the camera feed to your goggles. Antennas ensure the signal remains clear through obstacles and distance.

Frequency 5.8GHz
Output 1W+
